Cosmic Paradox

I wrote this track thinking about our place in space, lone and distant from anything that could resemble life.

One of my more evocative tracks, this track begins very lonesome and nostalgic, crescendoing into a beautiful middle section, before devolving into chaos and confusion, followed by a flowing jam, and ending with a return to our nostalgic origins. This track focused on transitions, development, and deliberate production choices.
